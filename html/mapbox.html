Instructions

Press "Up arrow" to move forward
Press "Down Arrow" to move backward
Press "Left Arrow" or "Right Arrow" to turn left and right respectively.
Press "1" or "2" to pitch down and pitch up respectively.
Press "3" or "4" to yaw right and yaw left respectively.
Press "8" to stop the plane.
